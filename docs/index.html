  • star fall
    Children have fun while they learn reading, phonics, and math. It has educational movies games, books, songs and more for children ages pre k-3rd grade.


  • Bloom-Art
    Mixed instruments and artwork


  • Brains on!
    Podcast uses science and history to explore questions about the world


  • Khan Academy
    Access free math, science and computer programming courses from KG through High school. The kids should see this www.thekidsshouldseethis.com Offers more than 4000 eclectic children’s videos. It is also entertaining for adults


  • Mystery Science
    Features video lessons for elementary school children and offers hands-on activities.

    A free programming community from MIT that gives students a chance to create interactive stories games and animation.
